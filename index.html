  • We became, parents, grandparents, great-grandparents, 13 nurses- (hospital, public health, educators, quality management, utilization and case management), several teachers (elementary to Graduate School), pastors, missionaries, civil and chemical engineers, farmers, foresters, loggers, fire fighters, volunteers, secretaries, musicians, dentists, dental hygienists, a chiropractor, physical therapists, small business owners and managers, bakers, long-haul truckers, a CPA’s, a mayor and civic leader, a mathematician, health care aid, welder, industrial arts teacher, hospital administrator and federal compliance and contracting officer representative….list not complete yet!!
