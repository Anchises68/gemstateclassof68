About Us

  • 114 classmates attended during our 4 years in high school 1964-1968.
  • Out of the 65 in the graduating Senior Class, 61 are surviving today.  We have lost the following Classmates:  Dorothy Wilcox Ammonson, Tony Beam, Verla Rollins Collett, Dennis Mack.  Two attending classmates that have passed away are Sherri McCunn and Vernon Michael.
  • Updated information was obtained on 46 (75%) of our 61 surviving classmates.  National average is 25% of the graduating class are  updated at the 50th reunion.
  • Eight of our classmates married their high school sweethearts:  John and Sharon (Moore) Hamel, Jerry and Pam (Franklin) Loop, Karen (Jaeger) and Randy Andregg, Tony and Patty (Lovell)Beam, Sue (Dousay) and Dennis Mack and Don and Sue (Foster)Vietz
  • We became parents, grandparents, great-grandparents.  Our occupations include 13 nurses(specializing in hospital, public health, mental health, educators, quality, utilization and case management,) several teachers (teaching elementary through Graduate School), pastors, counselors, missionaries, civil and chemical engineers, farmers, foresters, loggers, fire fighters, volunteers, secretaries, musicians, dentists, dental hygienists, a chiropractor, physical therapists, small business owners and managers, bakers, long-haul truckers, a CPS, a mayor and civic leader, a mathematician, health care aid, welder, Industrial Arts teacher, hospital adminstrators, a federal compliance and contracting officer representative.  Quite a number of our class continue to work or have started new occupations and hobbies.  Many are very engaged in the lives of their children, grandchildren, great-grandchildren.  In other words, we continue to thrive, contribute and serve.
  • Our class song was written by 2 classmates, Lavonne Grant Lee and Sherlyn Ferrera Samaan.
